Medivault

Medivault is a digital health card solution. Every person’s health data is securely stored in an app and encoded into a QR code for quick access and sharing. Perfect for hospitals, clinics, and personal medical records.

How it works:
  • Each user’s health data is stored in the Medivault app (frontend & backend).
  • The app generates a QR code containing encrypted health data.
  • Medical professionals can scan the QR code to instantly access the user’s health info.
