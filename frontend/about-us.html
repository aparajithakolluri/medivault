WE ARE MEDIVAULT

MediVault is a patient-centric health platform designed to streamline emergency medical access and long-term care management. It integrates QR-based health cards, secure data storage, and real-time synchronization to empower individuals and healthcare providers with instant access to critical medical information.

  • QR Health Cards for emergency responders and doctors
  • Smart Intake Forms for efficient diagnosis
  • Encrypted Medical Repository for secure storage
  • AI-Ready Health Records for predictive care
  • Multilingual Emergency Access for global usability
  • Data-Driven Insights for research and analytics

Impact & Vision: MediVault aims to serve over 100 million individuals managing chronic conditions or seeking peace of mind through organized health records. By bridging the gap between fragmented data and precision care, it transitions healthcare from reactive treatment to proactive prevention.

My Role: As the lead developer and strategist, I designed and implemented MediVault’s core architecture, including QR integration, UI/UX transitions, and backend synchronization. I focused on accessibility, emergency responsiveness, and scalable infrastructure to ensure real-world impact.
